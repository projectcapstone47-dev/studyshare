About StudyShare

Our Mission

StudyShare is a simple, open-access platform designed to help engineering students collaborate and share knowledge. We believe that education is most effective when students help each other learn.

What You Can Do

  • Upload and share study materials (notes, assignments, presentations)
  • Browse materials by subject and semester
  • Create study groups for collaborative learning
  • Download helpful resources shared by peers and teachers

Supported File Types

You can upload the following file types:

  • PDF documents (.pdf)
  • Word documents (.doc, .docx)
  • PowerPoint presentations (.ppt, .pptx)
  • Images (.jpg, .jpeg, .png)

Maximum file size: 10MB per upload

Guidelines

  • Share only educational content
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property
  • Use clear, descriptive titles for your uploads
  • Be respectful and supportive of fellow students

Technology

StudyShare is built with:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Vanilla JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js with Express.js
  • Database: SQLite
  • File Storage: Local server storage

Contact & Feedback

Have suggestions or found a bug? We'd love to hear from you! This is a community-driven project, and your feedback helps make it better.
